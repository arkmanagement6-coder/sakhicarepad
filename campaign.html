Menstrual Hygiene Awareness Campaign

Adopt hygiene, build a healthy India.

Under the Menstrual Hygiene Awareness Campaign, SakhiHub goes village to village to inform women and girls about period hygiene, sanitary pad use, infection prevention, safe disposal, and health awareness.

Campaign Objectives

  • Breaking the silence and taboos.
  • Educating on correct usage of pads.
  • Promoting infection prevention.

Topics Covered

  • Period Hygiene & Health
  • Safe Disposal Methods
  • Nutrition during menstruation

Field Activity

  • Village Awareness Camps
  • School/College Sessions
  • Door-to-door counseling
