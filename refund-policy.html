Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Membership Fees

The membership fee of ₹100 is a one-time contribution towards women's empowerment initiatives and is generally **non-refundable** once processed.

2. Product Returns

Due to the hygienic nature of our primary product (Sanitary Pads), we do not accept returns or offer refunds for opened or used products. If you receive a damaged product, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery with proof.

3. Cancellations

Orders can only be cancelled before they are dispatched. Once an order is handed over to our delivery partners, cancellation is not possible.

4. Process for Refund

In case of valid refund claims (e.g., payment failure but amount deducted), the refund will be processed back to the original payment method within 7-10 working days.
