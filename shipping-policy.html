Shipping Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Delivery Coverage

We currently deliver our products across India, focusing primarily on rural and semi-urban areas where our Sakhi network is active.

2. Shipping Times

Standard delivery takes 5-7 working days. In remote locations, it may take up to 10 working days.

3. Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are calculated at checkout based on the delivery location and order volume. We offer free shipping on bulk orders for community groups.

4. Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an SMS or WhatsApp notification with the tracking details.

5. Delivery Partner Program

Many of our deliveries are handled by local Sakhi Hub Delivery Partners, supporting local employment.
